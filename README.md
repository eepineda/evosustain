# EvoSustain

EvoSustain is a digital toolkit designed to analyze, manage, and
optimize sustainability-focused projects through data-driven insights,
intelligent workflows, and advanced resource planning.\
The platform enables organizations to improve operational efficiency,
reduce environmental impact, and enhance decision-making by leveraging
cloud technology, automation, and analytics.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🌍 Vision

To accelerate the transition toward smarter, more sustainable cities and
organizations by transforming traditional operations into connected,
data-driven ecosystems.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🎯 Objectives

-   Digitalize sustainability and operational processes
-   Improve efficiency in resource utilization and logistics
-   Reduce environmental impact and emissions
-   Enable real-time visibility and decision-making
-   Provide measurable sustainability insights and KPIs

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Service Request Management

-   Digital service requests from generators (e.g., stores, facilities,
    organizations)
-   Real-time communication with operators
-   Automated workflows and notifications

### 2. Intelligent Planning & Optimization

-   Route optimization for collections and field services
-   Resource allocation (drivers, vehicles, equipment)
-   Scheduling and workload balancing

### 3. Data Analytics & Reporting

-   Operational dashboards and KPIs
-   Sustainability metrics and environmental impact analysis
-   Historical performance tracking

### 4. Cloud-Native Architecture

-   Scalable cloud infrastructure
-   Secure data storage and processing
-   High availability and resilience

### 5. Integration Capabilities

-   APIs for third-party systems
-   Integration with IoT devices and sensors
-   ERP and enterprise system connectivity

### 6. Security & Compliance

-   Role-based access control
-   Audit trails and monitoring
-   Compliance-ready architecture
