Pilot learnings: fewer miles, lower CO₂

Feb 20, 2026 · Impact

Operational efficiency is sustainability in action.

What we measured

  • Reduced mileage per route (optimised stop order)
  • Better workload balance across drivers
  • Faster response time for ad-hoc pickup requests

Why it matters

Fewer miles means lower fuel consumption and lower emissions—and it improves service reliability, reducing rework and repeat trips.
