# London TV feature: EvoSustain and smarter urban waste logistics

London TV published a short feature about **EvoSustain** and our mission to modernise **urban waste and recycling collection** through technology, data analysis, and smarter operational planning.

## What the article highlights
- The opportunity to improve efficiency in waste collection operations.
- **Route optimisation** and better planning to reduce mileage and emissions.
- The importance of operational visibility and more connected workflows.
- EvoSustain is currently in the **Proof of Concept (PoC)** phase and open to collaborations and pilots.
