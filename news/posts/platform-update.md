# Platform development update: smarter planning and dispatch

We’re improving the EvoSustain workflow so operations teams can move from manual coordination to a connected, data-driven process.

> The goal is simple: fewer calls and emails, fewer miles driven, clearer accountability, and measurable impact.

## What’s new
- **Structured request intake** — stores submit pickup requests with type, volume and time window.
- **Better planning** — operators group stops and prioritise jobs based on capacity and constraints.
- **Clearer dispatch** — faster driver assignment and better operational visibility.

## Why it matters
Optimised planning reduces idle time and mileage—cutting fuel use, cost, and CO₂ emissions while improving service reliability and traceability.
