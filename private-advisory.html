Debt support ¬∑ UK legal options

Struggling with debt? There are legal ways to regain control.

The UK provides legal mechanisms that can pause payments, stop interest and protect you while you seek a long-term solution.

  • IVA: Reduced repayments and frozen interest.
  • DRO: Low-cost option for low income and small debts.
  • Bankruptcy: Last-resort reset when repayment is impossible.
  • DMP: Negotiated affordable monthly payments.
  • Breathing Space: 60-day legal protection period.
